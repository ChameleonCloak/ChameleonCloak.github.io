Chameleon Cloak Privacy Policy

This document is in revision, and is not binding

Effective date:

This privacy policy explains how the chameleon cloak research project group collects, uses, and discloses data about you. This privacy policy applies during the use of any instance of a chameleon cloak extension tool.
This policy may change periodically to comply with regulations. We may notify users of updates to the policy. We encourage checking this policy regularly to stay informed on the use of your data.

Who Owns Our Tools

Information We Collect and How

We collect data about network packets passing through your browser to known advertiser domains. This includes the website this advertiser request was made from, the domain of the advertisers, the intermediary source of the request, the url of the request, the data of the request, and the timestamp of the request.
All of this data is collected from the chrome webRequest api from the Ad-Detector extension.
This data is collected into a database by the backend, of which snapshots are taken to perform research about advertiser behaviour, patterns, and issues by the chameleon cloak research group.

How We Share Data

We do not share or sell your data outside of the chameleon cloak research group, and we do not use the data for any gain besides that of the research done on it.

Your Rights

By downloading and configuring the extension, you consent to our data collection, but under but certain laws entitle you to have a level on control and information about this data. We can not give a summary of your data from our dataset, due to it being anonymized, but you may store the data locally to review your own data.
